Belt conveyor systems for mines

One of the most common use of Belt conveyor systems is transportation of raw materials from mines to plants. But now a lot of companies started use conveyors in other fields? for example is food industry.

One of the world's longest belt conveyors is located in Western Sahara. Its length is 98 km. It transports phosphate stones from the CRAA mine in the port city of El Ayoun. From there, cargo ships transport phosphates to different countries where they are used in fertilizer production. Conveyor belt transports 2000 tons of breed per hour.
In the state of Arizona (USA), 10.5 km long conveyor line with a capacity of 1260 t / h is used for delivering from the place of extraction to the loading station. It consists of three passes with two 660 kW drive actuators. The first one has become the cargo raised to a height of 229 m, the second with a drive of 358 kW horizontally, the third takes coal and raises it to the height of 2.5 m.
