Mathematical model of Melnikova and Mathematical model of Dmitrieva

In her scientific work, Melnikova explores the issues of controlling the speed of tape conveyors depending on the load on the belt, which was repeatedly considered by a number of scientists. Also she takes into account the angle of the conveyor. In order to implement the proposed method of automatic control of the conveyor line, a loaded conveyor should be stopped with subsequent start-up, which may lead to a rupture of the belt. Therefore, the second task of Melnikova's research is to establish the regularities of a trouble-free launch of a loaded conveyor. The results of her research are presented in the figures below. As can be seen from the graphs, only when adjusting the speed of horizontal belt conveyors does the power consumed by the electric drive decrease, which means that the electric power consumption also decreases.
The mathematical model of Dmitrieva is based on the principle of dividing the contour of a tape into a number of sections. The construction of the mathematical model is based on the second-order Lagrange method. As a result, we obtained a system of equations and transient processes with respect to the velocities of the common coordinates. The graphs correspond to the acceleration mode and the operating mode of the conveyor with a constant speed.

Mathematical model of Razumnyi and Mathematical model of Chermalyh

Today, the main problem of coveyor transport as an object of control is the unevenness of the freight flow. This problem leads to an excessive demolition of the conveyor belt and an inefficient use of drive power. To eliminate the unevenness of the cargo flow in the work of Razumnyi it is proposed to simulate the flow of coal on the main assembly conveyor and to analyze the main characteristics.
Based on the results of the Chermalyh, the velocity and loading diagrams were obtained, which are shown below. The drawings illustrate the realization of the idea of ​​supporting the constant loading of conveyor belts by adjusting the speed of the drive engine depending on the magnitude of the cargo flow. Energy indicators (power, high power factor and efficiency) show the effectiveness of the use of a frequency-controlled electric drive for a belt conveyor.

Changing the statistical characteristics of freight flows with the help of an adjustable drive affects the electricity consumption of mine conveyors. Prokuda in his work determined the effect of the adjustable speed of the tape and the characteristics of the flow of traffic to the consumption of the conveyor in connection with the introduction of CRP on the main transport of coal mines. The obtained result allows to assert about more full use of the energy efficiency reserve when adjusting the speed of the installed conveyors.
