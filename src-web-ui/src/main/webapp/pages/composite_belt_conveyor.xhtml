Belt conveyor systems for mines

One of the most common use of Belt conveyor systems is transportation of raw materials from mines to plants. Surely coal mines occupy the biggest niche in the use of conveyor transport, but there are a lot of underground and overground pits, where conveyor systems are introduced.
Liming Heavy Indusrty is one of the hugest conveyor manufaturers. Liming Co. LTD exists sinse 1987. Their conveyor systems function in more than 140 countries of the world. The most striking examples are conveyors in Chile at an iron ore mine and in the Philippines for the transportation of granite. The given systems consist of 6-7 elements and carry capacity up to 400 tons per hour. In addition to the central main conveyor, the systems also have boot, drain and return conveyors.
Another one remarkable Belt Conveyor System functions in the underground gypsum mine in Novomoskovk, Russia. The depth of the mine is 130 meters. In the mine there are 5 branches, the total length of which is 500 km. The capacity of the conveyor system is more than 900 tons of material per hour. It should be noted that in addition to belt conveyorы, the mine is equipped with reversible, suspended and vertical conveyors.
As an example of big and powerful Conveyor system is able to bring the huge transport system in the USA on the gold mine. One of its contrasts is location - this conveyor system is disposed overground. The total length of the conveyor system is about 3 km. It consists of 7 different kinds of conveyors, including crushing, unloading, tank and 2 recycling conveyors for processing. The total capacity of the system is 1000 tons per hour.

Belt conveyor systems for storaged and warehouses

Another application of belt conveyor systems is in storages and warehouses. All largest delivery companies as UPS, FedEx, Amazon and so on use belt conveyor systems on their stocks. Remarkable that different repositories equipped with hundreds kilometers of conveyors.
The biggest Amazon coveyor system is located in Dupon, USA. One of the most prominent and most well-known conveyor systems is the American Amazon conveyor network. It is located in a warehouse in DuPont, USA. It should be noted that the area of ​​this ordering center is more than 90 thousand square meters, and the conveyor system with a total length reaches 15 kilometers. All conveyors operating in the Amazon repository are different in length, bending parameters (different turns, etc.). In addition, packages can move from the bottom up and vice versa, rebuilding to shift if necessary. Thanks to the technologies implemented, each box is photographed and scanned in certain parts of the system.
UPS storage has propably the biggest belt conveyor system amount all delivery companies. Total length of the system is 155 miles or 250 km. Each hour 416 000 of packages and mails are handled here. Generally this UPS conveyor system can process about 1.5 million packages per night. It should be noted that almost the whole process in the warehouses of Amazon and UPS is automated, which allows to significantly reduce the processing time of packages. At the point of handling UPS parcels, the person touches the load only twice, another work is left to the technique.
In 2017, in Kyiv, Ukraine, NovaPoshta company opened grand storage with enormous belt conveyor system. One of these conveyors is currently operating in Ukraine, in particular, in Kiev at the new "Kyiv Innovation Terminal" of the "New Mail" company. KIT is capable of handling 350 thousand parcels per day, equivalent to 400 trucks. The conveyor system is also equipped with a barcode scanner whose throughput is 2.3 units per second (from 200,000 parcels per day)
