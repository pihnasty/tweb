The results of simulation

As a result of the analysis of the work of the district transport system, a new way of automatic control of the conveyor line with intermediate bunkers is developed, which consists in regulating the operation of the system elements depending on the current state of the system. Below is a description of the possible states of the transport system and the reactions of its elements in these situations. 1 Normal operation of the plot. The combine works with nominal feed rate and performance, the conveyor also operates at nominal speed, the bunker operates in transit mode. 2 Emergency mode (accident at the trunk conveyor and its stop). In this case, it is possible to increase the work cycle of the site at the time of filling the hopper. With the help of the coordinating device, the hopper is transferred to the accumulating mode, the feed speed of the combine is reduced and, accordingly, the productivity, the speed of the conveyor is also reduced. The work continues until the bunker fills in completely. 3 Overflow mode. After filling the hopper, the coordinating device turns off the combine, turns off the loaded conveyor. The system is in this state until the failure of the main conveyor has been restored. 4 Post-accident mode of operation of the site. The inclusion of a main conveyor allows the bunker to open for the delivery of cargo. Also, a local belt conveyor and harvester are included at the same time at a reduced speed. The plot works at these speeds until the empty hopper is empty. The district conveyor and the combine are then converted to a nominal speed.
